Carbon Emisson Calculator

This project aims to develop a website or application that measures the carbon footprints of buildings by utilizing a comprehensive database. By incorporating custom values for various building types and features—such as flooring and fittings—and considering different climatic zones, we can provide accurate emissions assessments. This initiative emphasizes ecological conservation, promoting sustainable practices in construction and design. By facilitating access to this valuable data, we hope to enhance awareness and encourage efforts to reduce carbon footprints, ultimately contributing to natural protection and increasing the project's potential for recognition as a leading solution in environmental sustainability.
